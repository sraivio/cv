Default styles for season days & nights:

# Spring

Spring Day (07:00–20:00):
Background should have a soft blue sky, fluffy pale clouds, bright sun, gentle green hills, and small spring flowers in pixel‑art style.
The center panel background should be a pale spring green, borders thick and medium spring‑green, and text a dark warm brown (not black).

Spring Night (20:00–07:00):
Background should show a deep indigo sky with pastel stars, a pale moon, and dark softened spring greenery in pixel‑art style.
The center panel background should be a muted purple‑green, borders thick and lilac‑toned, and the font should be a soft light mint.

# Summer

Summer Day (07:00–20:00):
Background should have a bright blue sky, white clouds, sun, green grass, and small summery details in pixel‑art style.
The center panel background color is #f5e5ab, all borders should be thick and color #d88c35, and the font should be very dark brown, close to black but not black.

Summer Night (20:00–07:00):
Background should have a deep violet sky, stars, moon, night clouds, and a soft nighttime landscape in pixel‑art style.
The center panel background color is #41036b, all borders should be thick and color #a666d1, and the font should be a very light violet.


# Autumn

Autumn Day (07:00–20:00):
Background should show warm autumn colors: golden sky, soft beige clouds, orange and red foliage, and earthy hills in pixel‑art style.
The center panel background should be a warm tan, borders thick and burnt‑orange, and the text a deep brown.

Autumn Night (20:00–07:00):
Background should have a deep aubergine night sky, warm moonlight, sparse stars, and dark leafy silhouettes in pixel‑art style.
The center panel background should be a dark pumpkin‑brown, borders thick and warm amber, and the font a pale peach.

# Winter

Winter Day (07:00–20:00):
Background should have a cold, light icy‑blue sky with pale clouds, snow‑covered ground, and soft winter sunlight in pixel‑art style.
The center panel background should be icy pastel blue, borders thick and crisp cold blue, and the text a dark bluish‑gray.

Winter Night (20:00–07:00):
Background should show a deep dark‑blue winter night sky, bright white moon, crisp stars, and snowy terrain reflecting moonlight in pixel art.
The center panel background should be midnight blue, borders thick and icy blue, and the font should be a very light blue‑white.
